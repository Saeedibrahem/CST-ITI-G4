Welcome to MobileExpress

Dubai Phone is a leading retailer of electronic devices in Egypt, known for its extensive network of branches across the country, bringing it closer to customers wherever they are. With a commitment to offering the latest technologies, Dubai Phone has become a preferred destination for tech enthusiasts and shoppers seeking the best electronic products.

Over the years, we've grown into a trusted destination for smartphones, accessories, and expert advice. Our journey has been fueled by our commitment to quality products, competitive prices, and exceptional customer service.

Today, we serve thousands of customers across the country, both online and through our expanding network of retail stores, but we've never lost sight of our core values and the personal touch that made us successful in the first place.
